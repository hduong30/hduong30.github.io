Poems / Literature / Music

Poems

A poem’s content is what the poem is about and what the author includes in the lines. This could be dialogue, descriptions, meditations on a specific theme, and more. A poem is defined by its content. Without content, there is no poem. It is everything that the author includes in their verse, except the form.

Literature

The content is everything the author includes in their book, story, essay, or poem. This ranges from the characterization to plot, setting, and resolution. The only things not included in content are the elements of form or how the literary work is structured. Some of the many elements of content in literature are dialogue, setting, exposition, conflict, plot, characterization, resolution, moral, theme, and resolution.
